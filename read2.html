About My Village

My village name is Hardoi Gujar and my District name is Jalaun.Which is 100 km from Jhansi in Uttar Pradesh.In my village, the name of 7000 people comes in the voting list.The name of the sarpanch of my village is Raju Ahirwar.He is the sarpanch of our village for the last two five years.There are two big playgrounds in our village but there is no sports facility in them.There are 4 government schools in our village, but teachers never come to those schools, because of this, there is no education in those schools.Medical facilities are fine in our village.There is a water tank and a powerhouse in my village, so that we do not have any problem related to water and electricity power.Caste religion and untouchability are considered very much in the village.Caste religion and untouchability are considered very much in the village, but in the midst of our new generation, all this is rarely seen.We help each other in our village and all the people live in harmony with each other.Me and my friends helped the people coming from other cities during the lockdown period and we also gave food to the passers-by coming out of the village and arranged for them to rest by opening empty school rooms and in between we also followed covid 19 protocol.
